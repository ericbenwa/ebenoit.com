Howdy, I'm Eric Benoit, Creative Director at GoInvo, a healthcare design company in Arlington, Massachusetts.

I design delightful experiences for large-scale, complex, digital healthcare products that are destined to ship, and with a positive impact on Planet Earth.

When designing, I collaborate with:

  • Executives; to guide and provide vision while understanding the industry, business, and product.
  • Customers; to learn about their behavior, goals, pain, and translate that into a product they'll enjoy.
  • Engineers; to understand the technology, prototype, and guide design into code.

I've designed healthcare software for:

  • Clinical Decision Support
  • Electrocardiography (Patent US8620418 B1)
  • Electronic Consent
  • Genetic Reporting
  • Hospital Coding
  • In Vitro Fertilization
  • Patient Health Records
  • Public Health
  • Telemedicine

In my spare time I play hockey and tinker with Arduino.
